Gameplay

Part city-builder, part tower defence, part puzzle, set inside a sleek cyberpunk world of glowing cables and live packet traffic.

Build

Place routers, switches, access points, and servers onto a 4,000 × 4,000 unit scrollable, zoomable canvas.

Connect

Wire your equipment together following real networking rules — ISP to router, router to everything else.

Manage

Watch live utilisation indicators on every node. Upgrade overloaded infrastructure before it fails and takes the network down.

Survive

Devices keep spawning and traffic keeps growing. Too many critical failures and it's game over — NETWORK DOWN.

Score

Earn points for every connected device, byte of throughput, and second of uptime. Chase your high score across endless sessions.

Cyberpunk Aesthetic

Nodes glow cyan when healthy, amber under load, and red when critical. Live packet sprites race along connections in real time.

Equipment

Four types of infrastructure to design and expand your network.

Router

Your core backbone. Connects directly to the ISP and routes traffic between network segments. Upgradeable up to level 5.

Switch

High-throughput wired hub for servers, desktops, and other infrastructure. Upgradeable up to level 5.

Access Point

Wireless node that auto-detects nearby client devices within its range. Upgradeable up to level 5.

Server

High-capacity processing node that absorbs heavy workloads from demanding devices.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

UpLink does not collect personal data. No accounts, no analytics, no trackers.

Data Collection

UpLink is a self-contained game. It does not require an account, does not connect to external servers, and does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

  • Does not require account creation or sign-in
  • Does not collect or transmit personal information
  • Does not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers
  • Does not sell your data to third parties

Data Stored On-Device

UpLink stores game progress and settings locally on your device. This data is never transmitted off-device.

  • Game scores and session history are stored locally
  • Settings and preferences are stored locally
  • All data is removed when you delete the app
