Pulse

A diagnostics app designed to help verify the core functionality of your iPhone — from buttons to sensors. Built with SwiftUI and with complete privacy in mind.

Features

Verify your iPhone is working perfectly.

Button Tests

Test all physical buttons including volume, power, and silent switch to ensure they're responding correctly.

Touch Screen

Verify your display's touch sensitivity and multi-touch capabilities across the entire screen.

Speaker & Mic

Test your speakers and microphone to ensure audio input and output are working properly.

Sensors

Check accelerometer, gyroscope, compass, and other sensors for accurate readings.

Beautiful Design

A clean, intuitive interface built with SwiftUI that makes diagnostics simple and clear.

Privacy First

No accounts, no tracking, no data collection. All tests run locally on your device.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Pulse does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

Pulse is designed with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect any personal information from users. The app:

  • Does not require account creation or sign-in
  • Does not collect or store personal data
  • Does not track your usage or behavior
  • Does not use analytics or third-party trackers
  • Does not share any data with third parties

Diagnostic Tests

All diagnostic tests run entirely on your device. Test results:

  • Are processed entirely on your device
  • Are never uploaded to any servers
  • Are never stored or logged
  • Are not shared with anyone

Third-Party Services

Pulse does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data collection services. Your diagnostic experience is completely private.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page. Since we don't collect any data, we have no way to notify users directly—but our commitment to privacy will remain unchanged.
