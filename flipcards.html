Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

FlipCards does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

FlipCards is designed with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect any personal information from users. The app:

  • Does not require account creation or sign-in
  • Does not collect or store personal data
  • Does not track your usage or behavior
  • Does not use analytics or third-party trackers
  • Does not share any data with third parties

Your Flashcards

All flashcards and study sets you create are stored locally on your device. This data:

  • Is stored entirely on your device
  • Is never uploaded to any servers
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Can be deleted at any time by removing the app

Third-Party Services

FlipCards does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data collection services. Your study experience is completely private.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page. Since we don't collect any data, we have no way to notify users directly—but our commitment to privacy will remain unchanged.
